# LocalFixKenya - Complete Workflow Testing Guide\n\n## 🎬 Quick Start\n\n### 1. Start the Development Server\n\n```bash\ncd c:\\Users\\joe\\Documents\\local-fix-kenya-appp\nnpm run dev\n```\n\nServer runs at `http://localhost:3000`\n\n---\n\n## ✅ WORKFLOW 1: Apply for a Job (Worker)\n\n### Step 1: Browse Jobs\n1. Visit `http://localhost:3000/jobs`\n2. See list of available jobs\n3. Use search and category filters\n\n### Step 2: View Job Details\n1. Click any job card\n2. Go to `http://localhost:3000/jobs/[job-id]`\n3. See full details including:\n   - Job title, description\n   - Budget, location, duration\n   - Required skills\n   - Client information\n\n### Step 3: Apply for Job\n1. Fill in:\n   - **Cover Letter**: Explain your experience and why you're a good fit\n   - **Proposed Rate**: Your quoted price\n2. Click **\"Submit Application\"**\n3. See confirmation: \"Application submitted successfully\"\n\n### Step 4: Track Application\n1. Go to **Worker Dashboard** (`/dashboard/worker`)\n2. Look for job in the **\"Job Applications\"** section\n3. Status shows as **\"pending\"** initially\n\n### Step 5: Client Reviews (if logged in as client)\n1. Go to **Client Dashboard** (`/dashboard/client`)\n2. See your application in **\"Job Applications\"** section\n3. Click **\"View Details\"** to see your cover letter and proposed rate\n4. Click **\"Approve\"** or **\"Reject\"**\n\n### Step 6: Chat with Client (after approval)\n1. Go back to **Worker Dashboard**\n2. Click **\"Chat\"** button in job application entry\n3. Send/receive messages in real-time\n4. Chat persists for 1 hour then auto-deletes\n\n---\n\n## 📝 WORKFLOW 2: Post a Job (Client)\n\n### Step 1: Navigate to Post Job\n**Option A: From Dashboard**\n1. Go to **Client Dashboard** (`/dashboard/client`)\n2. Click **\"Post a job\"** button in sidebar or header\n\n**Option B: Direct URL**\n1. Visit `http://localhost:3000/jobs/post`\n\n### Step 2: Fill Job Form\nRequired fields:\n- **Title**: e.g., \"Fix leaking kitchen faucet\"\n- **Description**: Detailed requirements\n- **Category**: Choose from dropdown (Plumbing, Electrical, etc.)\n- **Location**: Where job needs to be done\n- **Budget**: Price in KES\n- **Budget Type**: Fixed or Hourly\n- **Duration**: One-time, Weeks, Months, Ongoing, or Custom\n- **Required Skills**: Select or create custom skills\n\n### Step 3: Submit\n1. Click **\"Post Job\"** button\n2. See confirmation: \"Job posted successfully!\"\n3. Redirected to `/jobs` page\n\n### Step 4: Verify Job Posted\n1. Job appears on Jobs Browse page\n2. Appears in **Client Dashboard** under \"My Jobs\"\n3. Email notifications sent to nearby providers\n\n### Step 5: Receive Applications\n1. Workers apply for your job\n2. Applications appear in **Client Dashboard**\n3. Click **\"View Details\"** to see worker info and rate\n4. Click **\"Approve\"** to move to booking stage\n\n### Step 6: Chat with Approved Worker\n1. Click **\"Chat\"** button in application entry\n2. Communicate details with worker\n\n---\n\n## 🔧 WORKFLOW 3: Create a Service (Worker)\n\n### Step 1: Navigate to Service Creation\n**Option A: From Dashboard**\n1. Go to **Worker Dashboard** (`/dashboard/worker`)\n2. Look for **\"Offer a Service\"** or similar link\n3. Click to go to `/services/offer`\n\n**Option B: Direct URL**\n1. Visit `http://localhost:3000/services/offer`\n\n### Step 2: Check Profile Requirements\nIf profile incomplete, complete:\n- Full Name\n- Email Address\n- Phone Number\n- Location\n\nThen retry creating service.\n\n### Step 3: Fill Service Form\n- **Service Name**: e.g., \"Plumbing Repair\"\n- **Description**: What you offer and experience\n- **Category**: Select from list\n- **Price**: Rate in KES\n- **Duration**: How long typically takes\n- **Location**: Where you operate\n\n### Step 4: Submit\n1. Click **\"Submit\"** or **\"Create Service\"**\n2. See confirmation: \"Service created successfully!\"\n3. Redirected to **Worker Dashboard**\n\n### Step 5: Verify Service Created\n1. See service in **Worker Dashboard** under \"Your Services\"\n2. Service appears on **Services Browse** page (`/services`)\n3. Real-time visible to clients\n\n### Step 6: Manage Service\n1. In Worker Dashboard, click service\n2. Toggle status between **\"Open\"** and **\"Closed\"**\n3. Update details\n4. Delete if needed\n\n---\n\n## 🛍️ WORKFLOW 4: Browse & Book Services (Client)\n\n### Step 1: Browse Services\n1. Visit `http://localhost:3000/services`\n2. See list of available services\n3. Use search and category filters\n\n### Step 2: Search & Filter\n- **Search Box**: Find by service name or provider\n- **Category Buttons**: Filter by service type\n- Results update in real-time\n\n### Step 3: View Service Card Details\nEach card shows:\n- Service name\n- Provider name and avatar\n- Provider tier badge\n- Location\n- Price (KES)\n- Duration\n- Description preview\n\n### Step 4: Book Service\n1. Click **\"View & Book\"** button\n2. Taken to provider profile (`/provider/[id]`)\n3. See full provider details and past work\n4. Click **\"Book Service\"** button\n\n### Step 5: Track Booking\n1. Go to **Client Dashboard** (`/dashboard/client`)\n2. See booking in **\"Recent Bookings\"** section\n3. Status shows as **\"pending\"** initially\n\n### Step 6: Wait for Approval\n1. Worker approves booking in their dashboard\n2. Status changes to **\"approved\"**\n3. Chat button becomes active\n\n### Step 7: Chat with Provider\n1. Click **\"Chat\"** button in booking entry\n2. Discuss schedule, details, payment\n3. Messages auto-delete after 1 hour\n\n### Step 8: Complete & Review\n1. After service completion\n2. Status changes to **\"completed\"**\n3. Click **\"Leave Review\"** button\n4. Rate (1-5 stars) and write review\n\n---\n\n## 📊 DASHBOARD WORKFLOWS\n\n### Worker Dashboard (`/dashboard/worker`)\n\n**Sections:**\n1. **Profile Header** - Your info, edit button, logout\n2. **Summary Cards** - Services, bookings, avg rating\n3. **Your Services** - Manage services you created\n4. **Available Jobs** - Browse & apply for jobs\n5. **Job Applications** - Track applications you sent\n6. **Bookings** - Manage service bookings\n7. **Reviews** - Client feedback\n8. **Sidebar**\n   - Quick stats\n   - Links to edit profile\n   - Logout button\n\n**Actions Available:**\n- Create new service → `/services/offer`\n- Browse jobs → `/jobs`\n- Apply for job → Click job card\n- Chat with clients → Click \"Chat\" button\n- View reviews → Scroll to Reviews section\n\n### Client Dashboard (`/dashboard/client`)\n\n**Sections:**\n1. **Profile Header** - Your info, edit button, logout\n2. **Summary Cards** - Jobs posted, bookings, reviews left\n3. **My Jobs** - Jobs you posted, applications received\n4. **Recent Bookings** - Services you booked\n5. **Sidebar**\n   - Quick stats\n   - Link to post job → `/jobs/post`\n   - Link to browse services → `/services`\n   - Link to dashboard\n   - Edit profile button\n   - Logout button\n\n**Actions Available:**\n- Post new job → `/jobs/post` or click button\n- Browse services → `/services`\n- Review applications → Click application entry\n- Approve/reject application → Click buttons\n- Chat with workers → Click \"Chat\" button\n- Book service → From `/services` browse page\n\n---\n\n## 🔐 AUTHENTICATION\n\n### First Time Setup\n1. Visit `http://localhost:3000`\n2. Click **\"Sign Up\"** or **\"Login\"**\n3. Use email/password authentication\n4. Complete profile setup\n5. Choose role: Worker, Client, or both\n\n### Role Selection\n- **Worker**: Can create services, apply for jobs\n- **Client**: Can post jobs, book services\n- **Both**: Can do everything\n\n### Logout\n1. From any dashboard: Click **\"Logout\"** button\n2. Session cleared\n3. Redirected to login page\n\n---\n\n## 🧪 TEST SCENARIOS\n\n### Scenario 1: Full Job Workflow\n1. **User 1 (Client)**: Create account, post job\n2. **User 2 (Worker)**: Create account, apply for job\n3. **User 1**: Approve application\n4. **User 2 & User 1**: Chat about job details\n5. **User 1**: Mark job complete, leave review\n\n### Scenario 2: Full Service Workflow\n1. **User 1 (Worker)**: Create account, offer service\n2. **User 2 (Client)**: Browse services, book User 1's service\n3. **User 1**: See booking notification, approve\n4. **User 1 & User 2**: Chat about service details\n5. **User 2**: Mark service complete, leave review\n\n### Scenario 3: Multiple Applications\n1. **User 1 (Client)**: Post job\n2. **User 2, 3, 4 (Workers)**: All apply\n3. **User 1**: Review all applications\n4. **User 1**: Approve preferred worker\n5. **User 1**: Reject others\n\n### Scenario 4: Profile Visibility\n1. **User 1 (Worker)**: Complete profile with skills\n2. **User 2 (Client)**: Post job requiring User 1's skills\n3. **User 1**: See relevant job in dashboard\n4. **User 1**: Apply and track status\n\n---\n\n## 🐛 TROUBLESHOOTING\n\n### Job/Service Not Appearing\n- Refresh page (Ctrl+R)\n- Wait a few seconds for real-time sync\n- Check profile is complete\n- Verify location matches\n\n### Can't Apply for Job\n- Ensure you're logged in as worker\n- Check profile is complete (name, location)\n- Verify job status is \"open\"\n- Try refreshing page\n\n### Chat Not Working\n- Ensure application/booking is \"approved\"\n- Check internet connection\n- Wait for real-time subscription to activate\n\n### Profile Not Updating\n- Click \"Save\" explicitly\n- Wait a few seconds\n- Refresh page to verify\n- Check for error messages\n\n---\n\n## 📱 URL REFERENCE\n\n| Feature
